Frequently Asked Questions

How much does solar cost in Texas?

Most residential systems vary based on energy usage, roof size, and equipment selection. We provide a customized proposal with projected savings and financing options.

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed in 1–3 days once permits and approvals are finalized. The full process typically takes a few weeks.

Do solar panels work during winter?

Yes. Solar panels generate electricity year-round and often perform efficiently in cooler temperatures when sunlight is available.

What happens during a power outage?

Standard grid-tied systems shut down for safety. With battery backup systems, critical circuits can remain powered during outages.

Will solar increase my home value?

Owned solar systems can make homes more attractive to buyers and may increase resale value while reducing ongoing utility costs.

Do solar panels work during cloudy weather?

Yes. Panels still produce electricity on cloudy days, although output is lower than during full sun conditions.

Can solar power my home during an outage?

A solar system paired with battery storage can provide backup power to selected appliances and essential circuits during outages.

What warranties are included?

Texas Sun Power offers comprehensive equipment and workmanship coverage, including long-term manufacturer warranties on major components.

How much roof space is required?

Roof space depends on your energy needs and panel efficiency. Our design team evaluates available roof area during the consultation process.
